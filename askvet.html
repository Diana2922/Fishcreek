Our dog,Sparky likes to eat whatever the kids are snacking on.Is it OK for the dog to each chocolate?
Chocolate is toxic for dogs. Please don't feed your dog chocolate. Try playing a game with your children — when you feed them people treats, they can feed Sparky dog treats.
